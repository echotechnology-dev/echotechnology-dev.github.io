Our Technology Approach

Echo Technology builds Surf Browser on top of ungoogled-chromium ‚Äî an open-source Chromium base without Google services.

Our focus is not on deep source-level patching, but on careful configuration, sane defaults, and predictable browser behavior.

Surf Browser is optimized for reduced background activity, lower resource usage, and strong compatibility with modern websites ‚Äî without added telemetry, advertising frameworks, or hidden online services.

We believe privacy should be practical. That is why Surf Browser avoids extreme changes that break websites or user workflows.

* Chromium and ungoogled-chromium are independent open-source projects used as technical foundations. Echo Technology does not control upstream development and focuses on transparent configuration, distribution, and user experience.

Default Search in Surf Browser

Search is a core part of everyday browsing. That is why Surf Browser uses a privacy-respecting search engine by default.

Surf Browser uses Presearch as its default search engine.
