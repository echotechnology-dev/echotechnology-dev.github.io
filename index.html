Our Technology Approach

Echo Technology builds its browsers on carefully selected Chromium-based foundations, choosing the right balance between compatibility, performance, and privacy.

Surf Browser is available in two editions: one based on Vanilla Chromium, focused on broad compatibility and stability, and another based on Ungoogled Chromium, designed for users who prefer a fully de-Googled, privacy-first experience.

Both editions are optimized for reduced background activity, minimal resource usage, and transparent behavior, without added telemetry, advertising frameworks, or hidden online services.

We believe modern users face different challenges online. That is why Echo focuses on flexible, purpose-driven builds, rather than a one-size-fits-all solution.

* Chromium and Ungoogled Chromium are independent open-source projects used as technical foundations. Echo Technology does not modify or control upstream Chromium development and focuses on transparent configuration, distribution, and user experience.

Default Search in Surf Browser

Search is a core part of everyday browsing. That is why Surf Browser uses a privacy-respecting search engine by default.

Surf Browser uses Presearch as its default search engine.

Private search powered by Presearch

Presearch is a decentralized search platform focused on privacy and transparency. It does not track users, build personal profiles, or sell behavioral data.

Presearch is a third-party search provider. Echo Technology is an independent project and is not owned or operated by Presearch.
